Rated 74th golf course in England, come and join us!

The Course

At Woodlands Heath Golf Club there is a full 18 hole course, which can be played as long as 7100 yards which is perfect for those who like a distance challenge. There are 5 different tees to choose from so you can choose your total course yardage on the day.

The Club House

On entrance to the golf club you will see a state of the art club house with a full dinning area with large tv displays showing live sports!

Memberships

We offer two types of members at Woodland Heath.
There is the full membership which allows for unlimited play. We also offer a points based membership, where you have a number of points to use over the course of the year.
Please contact us for more information.
